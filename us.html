About Us

Awesome Collection LLC is a family owned shoes and clothing store located in the heart of Brentwood, a suburb right outside the city of Pittsburgh. We realized the need of such a store in this area realizing the fact that not other stroes of simillar kind existed in this neighborhood. Customers in this neighborhood were forced to visit Century II or Southhills Village to find stuffs that we carry. To meet the demand of our local customers we thought of bringing a store of this kind in this area.
